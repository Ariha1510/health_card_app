The Nirantar Engine Technology

1. Compression Matrix

Stores records as tokenized variables (e.g. DIA,HYP for Diabetes and Hypertension) rather than verbose JSON to ensure the QR code stays scannable on standard phone cameras.

2. AES Symmetrical Shield

Encrypts serialized tokens locally using AES-256 with a global key. Protects patient details from general public scans while remaining decryptable by any authorized scanner app.

3. ABDM Alignment

Serves as a vital fail-safe, zero-network fallback for the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABHA), ensuring continuity in deep rural regions or high-transit construction camps.
