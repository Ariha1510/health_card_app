The Crisis 02 / 06

The Health Continuity Gap

✗

Medical History Reset

Migrant workers lose paper prescriptions, medical logs, and diagnosis records during seasonal travel between states.

✗

Connectivity Blindspots

Deep rural clinics, remote construction sectors, and transit camps lack stable, high-speed cellular networks.

✗

High-Risk Emergency Threats

Without records, doctors in new locations inadvertently prescribe drugs conflicting with unknown critical allergies.

💔

The Status Quo

Every relocation wipes the patient's record clean. Chronic treatments (Tuberculosis, Diabetes) reset, raising morbidity risks.

Architecture 04 / 06

Tokenized Compression System

Standard JSON text is too bulky for a QR code. We map fields to tiny, standardized tokens to minimize pixels.

Medical Field Uncompressed Standard (JSON) Nirantar Compressed Token Bytes Saved
Demographics {"name":"Ram Kumar", "age":34, "gender":"M"} Ram Kumar|34|M ~60%
Blood Group "O-Negative" O- ~85%
Chronic Diseases ["Diabetes", "Hypertension"] DIA,HYP ~75%
Vaccinations {"Tetanus": 2025, "Covid": 2022} T25,C22 ~70%
Serialized Pipeline: V1|Ram Kumar|34|M|O-|DIA,HYP|PCN|T25,C22
Minimizes QR version footprint to level 5 (37x37 grid) for optimal low-light scanning Nirantar Health Deck
Security & Verification 05 / 06

Local Symmetrical Encryption

🔒

AES-256 Symmetrical Shield

The serialized medical string is encrypted before compiling into QR pixels. Private health information cannot be read by commercial camera scanners.

🔑

Universal Clinic Secret

Authorized health workers log in once to save the decryption token locally. Scanner automatically decrypts scanning feeds on-the-fly.

💾

Worker Sovereignty

No central record database exists that can leak credentials. The worker owns the physical card containing their only record copy.

Scanning Protocol Flow

1
Scan encrypted QR code via HTML5 Reader
2
Extract AES ciphertext from image pixels
3
Decrypt locally using stored shared secret
4
Parse tokens and render full patient chart
Complies with HIPPA principles: absolute patient data ownership Nirantar Health Deck
